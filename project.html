Movie Ratings and Trends

Exploring audience preferences across genres and time

Introduction Visualizations Insights Conclusion

Introduction

This project analyzes trends in movie ratings across different genres and years, uncovering insights into audience preferences and how the quality of films has evolved over time.

Visualizations

Chart 1 | Overall Ratings by Genre

This bar chart compares the average audience ratings for different genres, showcasing which genres perform consistently well.

Chart 2 | Ratings Trends for Top Genres Over Time

This line chart tracks the evolution of average ratings for the top genres from 1990 to 2024, offering insights into changing audience preferences.

Chart 3 | Distribution of Ratings Across All Genres

The histogram visualizes the distribution of ratings across genres, showing the spread of audience reception.

Chart 4 | Success Rates of Genres

This bar chart displays the proportion of highly rated movies (vote average > 7) for each genre.

Insights

Key trends identified:

  • Drama and Documentary: Consistently high ratings highlight their wide appeal.
  • Animation: Steady improvement in ratings reflects advancements in storytelling.
  • Comedy and Horror: Most variability in ratings, suggesting polarizing audience reactions.

Conclusion

By exploring movie ratings across genres and time, this project provides valuable insights for filmmakers and streaming platforms. Future analyses could incorporate additional factors like demographics and revenue to build a more comprehensive picture.
