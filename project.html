Introduction

What drives audience preferences, and how have movie ratings evolved across genres over time? For making data-driven decisions, it is essential for the companies in the entertainment industry to understand the movie ratings and audience preferences. This analysis, conducted based on the dataset about popular movies from TMDb, delves into key indicators, including movie vote averages, genres, and release years. This study can be helpful for producers, marketers, and movie platforms in shaping their content strategies.

Data

The analysis is conducted based on an open API from TMDb. It is a comprehensive dataset that has key variables like vote averages, release dates, genres, and popularity scores. The data covers multiple decades, providing a comprehensive view of audience preferences throughout the timeframe.

Conclusion

The analysis highlights key insights into movie ratings, including trends across genres, audience preferences, and their evolution over time. These findings can guide companies in optimizing content strategies and improving streaming services.
