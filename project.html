Movie Ratings and Trends

Exploring audience preferences across genres and time

Introduction Visualizations Insights Conclusion

Introduction

This project analyzes trends in movie ratings across different genres and years, providing insights into audience preferences and the evolution of film quality over time.

Visualizations

Chart 1 | Overall Ratings by Genre

This bar chart illustrates the average audience ratings for different genres. It provides a clear comparison of how each genre performs in terms of audience satisfaction.

Chart 2 | Ratings Trends for Top Genres Over Time

This line chart tracks the evolution of average ratings for the top genres from 1990 to 2024. Use the dropdown and slider filters to explore specific genres and years.

Chart 3 | Distribution of Ratings Across All Genres

The histogram shows the distribution of ratings across all genres. It highlights how audience ratings are spread, with most clustering between 6 and 8.

Chart 4 | Success Rates of Genres

This bar chart shows the proportion of highly rated movies (vote average > 7) for each genre, emphasizing which genres consistently produce quality films.

Chart 5 | Average Ratings Over Time

This interactive chart displays how average ratings have changed over time for all genres. Use filters to analyze specific trends.

Insights

This project reveals several key trends:

  • Drama and Documentary are the highest-rated genres overall.
  • Animation has seen steady improvement in ratings over the years.
  • Comedy and Horror exhibit the most variability in ratings, reflecting polarizing audience reception.

Conclusion

By exploring the dynamics of movie ratings across genres and time, this project provides valuable insights for industry stakeholders. Future work could incorporate demographic data and explore revenue impacts alongside ratings.
